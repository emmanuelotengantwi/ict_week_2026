Theme for 2026

Innovation at Scale

Creating Opportunities Across Ghana's Digital Ecosystem

The 2026 theme, "Innovation at Scale: Creating Opportunities Across Ghana's Digital Ecosystem", is intentionally designed as a direct progression from the 2025 ICT Week Celebration theme, "Ghana as Africa's Digital Trade Hub: Innovation, Policy, and Partnerships for the Future."

The 2025 ICT Week Celebration positioned Ghana within the continental digital economy, focusing on how innovation, enabling policies, and strategic partnerships could establish the country as Africa's preferred hub for digital trade. That year's discussions centered on foundational enablers such as digital identity, digital payments, data exchange, digital trust frameworks, cross-border trade systems, and regulatory collaboration, laying the groundwork for Ghana's role in regional and pan-African digital commerce.

Building on these foundations, the 2026 theme shifts the narrative from positioning to execution and scale. Having articulated the vision of Ghana as a digital trade hub and strengthened the policy, trust, and partnership frameworks required to support that vision, the focus now turns to scaling innovation across the entire digital ecosystem, beyond trade-specific use cases and into all technology-driven sectors of the economy.
